Whether you’ve made the decision to attend Belmont or are still deciding, Be Belmont Day is the perfect opportunity for you to:

  • meet faculty, staff, current students and incoming freshmen;
  • learn about the freshman orientation process and the next steps in the financial planning process;
  • gain in-depth information about what interests you— from academic opportunities to student organizations.
 
 

Also enjoy lunch on campus and join us in the evening for the Best of the Best Showcase, a live student produced concert featuring some of the best music artists on Belmont’s campus. While you are here, check out the fun things to do in Nashville!
